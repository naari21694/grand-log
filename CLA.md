By submitting a contribution, you agree that:

1. **It's yours to give.** Your contribution is your original work, or you have the rights to submit it, and doing so doesn't violate anyone else's rights.
2. **You grant a broad license.** You grant the project maintainer (V Narendra Kumar) a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable license to use, reproduce, modify, and **sublicense** your contribution, including the right to license it under **both AGPL-3.0 and commercial/proprietary terms**.
3. **You keep your copyright.** You retain ownership of your contribution — this is a license, not an assignment.
4. **As-is.** You provide your contribution without warranties.

This is what lets the maintainer offer commercial licenses (the funding model) while everything stays open under AGPL-3.0 for everyone else.
