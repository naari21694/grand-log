# Governance

Grand Log is a young, single-maintainer project that wants to grow into a healthy crew.

## Roles
- **Maintainer / captain** — V Narendra Kumar (@naari21694). Final say on direction, merges, releases, and the commercial license.
- **Maintainers** (future) — trusted contributors granted merge rights, earned through quality PRs and helping others over time.
- **Contributors** — anyone with a merged PR. 🎉

## How decisions are made
- Small changes: a maintainer reviews and merges.
- Larger changes: open an issue or Discussion first, so we agree on the approach before code.
- Disagreements: discussed in the open; the maintainer breaks ties.

## Becoming a maintainer
Consistently good PRs + sound judgment in reviews + kindness → an invitation to the maintainers team.

## Why a CLA + dual license
So Grand Log stays **free and open forever** for the community, while a commercial license funds the work and stops anyone closing it off without giving back. See [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md).
